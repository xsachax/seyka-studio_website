About

Who?

Seyka Studio is an indie game development company based in Ottawa, Canada. Launched in early 2022, it's a place where all gamers can enjoy simple and fun video games without having to break the bank.

Why?

Our mission is to make free online games available to everyone all around the world. We want to grow the gaming community by creating environments where players can spend time honing their talents.

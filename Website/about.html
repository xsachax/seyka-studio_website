So Far, One

Seyka Studio is a non-profit indie game development studio based in Ottawa, Canada. We don't have many games yet, but we're establishing a foundation for the next important gaming community.
Our goal is to learn as much as possible about the current video game amateurs to offer them the greatest free online experience they will ever have.

I've always been passionate about the online world, so I started making games in my free time. Every day became a new learning experience.
